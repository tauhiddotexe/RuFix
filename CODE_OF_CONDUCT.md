# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We expect all participants in the RuFix community to uphold this code of conduct.

---

## Expected Behavior

✅ **Be Respectful**
- Treat all community members with kindness and respect
- Welcome different perspectives and experiences
- Listen actively and consider others' viewpoints

✅ **Be Inclusive**
- Create a welcoming environment for everyone
- Support underrepresented groups in tech
- Use inclusive language (avoid gendered terms, be mindful of cultural differences)

✅ **Be Constructive**
- Provide helpful feedback and code reviews
- Focus on ideas, not personal attacks
- Help newer contributors grow and learn

✅ **Be Professional**
- Keep discussions on-topic and productive
- Respect intellectual property and licenses
- Give credit where credit is due

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

❌ **Harassment & Discrimination**
We do not tolerate:
- Offensive comments related to gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, mental illness, neuro(a)typicality, physical appearance, body size, age, race, or religion
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- Harassment of any kind, public or private

❌ **Trolling & Bad Faith**
- Deliberately derailing discussions
- Posting irrelevant or inflammatory content
- Repeated rule violations after warnings

❌ **Violence & Threats**
- Threatening violence or harm
- Glorifying violence
- Incitement to violence

❌ **Spam & Self-Promotion**
- Unsolicited commercial advertising
- Spamming issues or PRs
- Unrelated self-promotion without community context

---

## Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience a violation of this code of conduct:

1. **Document the incident** — Note the date, time, people involved, and what happened
2. **Report privately** — Email [tauhidch2003@gmail.com](mailto:tauhidch2003@gmail.com) with details
3. **Include context** — Links to comments, issues, or conversations if possible
4. **Remain confidential** — Your report will be handled with discretion

**We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly.**

---

## Enforcement

Community leaders will:
- Review reported violations fairly and objectively
- Maintain confidentiality of reporters
- Take appropriate action (warnings, temporary bans, permanent removal)
- Communicate decisions transparently

### Consequences

Violations may result in:
1. **Verbal Warning** — For first-time minor violations
2. **Temporary Suspension** — For repeated or moderate violations (7-30 days)
3. **Permanent Ban** — For severe or repeated violations

---

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to:
- All GitHub issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Code comments and commit messages
- External channels (Twitter, Discord, etc.) when representing the RuFix project
